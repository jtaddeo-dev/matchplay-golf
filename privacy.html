Privacy Policy

MatchPlay · Effective March 28, 2026

Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the MatchPlay app (the “App”), created by Justin Taddeo. This service is provided “as is.” By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information Collection and Use

For a better experience, while using our App, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information. The information that we request will be retained on your device and is not collected by the developer in any way.

The App does use third party services that may collect information used to identify you.

Third Party Services

MatchPlay utilizes the following third party service providers:

  • Google AdMob: Used to serve advertisements within the App. AdMob may collect and use your advertising ID (IDFA) and device information to show personalized or non-personalized ads. You can view Google’s Privacy Policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Apple In-App Purchases: Used to handle the “Remove Ads” transaction. All financial data and transaction processing are handled securely by Apple. The developer does not have access to your credit card or banking information.

Location Data

The App requests access to your device’s location to provide a “Nearby Courses” list for easier scorecard selection.

  • Use of Location: Your location is used strictly to filter and identify golf courses in your immediate vicinity.
  • Storage: This location data is processed locally on your device and is never stored, shared, or transmitted to the developer’s own servers.

Data Storage

All match-related data (player names, scores, wagers, and match history) is saved locally on your Apple device. We do not use any cloud syncing services (such as Firebase or CloudKit). If you delete the App, this local data will be permanently removed.

Security

We value your trust. Because your core match data is stored locally, the security of your information is tied to the security of your Apple device. We recommend using a passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID to protect your information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
